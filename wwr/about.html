Que Tuanis! Rafting

Our goal at "Que Tuanis" Rafting is to ensure you enjoy the best experience with our different adventure rivers and various packages so you can have fun with your friends and family.

History

Thanks to the various rivers that flow through the province of Heredia, our founder decided to take advantage of these natural waters, turning these different channels into a means of enjoyment for everyone and for those seeking a little adrenaline rush.

We have named the different rivers after distinctive features of Costa Rican culture, such as popular sayings, national symbols, and even legends, so that you can feel and enjoy your experience in Costa Rica.
